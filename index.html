Signals

Signals

These are the operating signals I take seriously because they usually appear before a bigger breakdown.

  • Variance beyond ±5% on key KPIs or KRIs
  • Manual rework that repeats across more than one cycle
  • Unclear ownership in reports, approvals, or settlement
  • Slow handoffs between planning, execution, and verification

Operating Thesis

Operating Thesis

My current thesis is simple: good systems make trust visible and execution lighter.

  • Clarity lowers cost faster than speed hides it.
  • A routine only matters when another person can run it without confusion.
  • Automation should reduce work, not add another layer of management.
  • A founder dashboard is useful only when it leads directly to a decision or action.
