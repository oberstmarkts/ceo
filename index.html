About

From finance and field operations to AI-enabled execution.

My academic route moved from a self-directed Bachelor of Business Administration to an MBA in Financial Management at Pusan National University, followed by doctoral coursework. My operating route ran in parallel: entrepreneurship, finance, publishing, commerce, and process design.

Service as a Republic of Korea Navy officer reinforced how I think about safety, procedure, control, and accountability. Since then, I have applied that discipline to business operations—especially in revenue structure, reporting, standard operating procedures, cash flow visibility, and execution quality.

Today I focus on founder-level system design: turning fragmented work into repeatable workflows through evidence standards, KPI logic, automation, and decision-ready reporting.
